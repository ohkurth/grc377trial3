Welcome to Take-it-Table

"World Class Portable Party Tables"

Take-It-Table is a locally owned startup company in San Luis Obispo that specializes in portable and customizable party equipment. The company is known for its revolutionary and reliable table structure that can be easily disassembled and packed into a travel-safe backpack for lightweight, on-the-go use. Take-it-Tables have one purpose: fitting your lifestyle. This means that wherever you go, whatever you do, you can bring a table with you for whatever you may need it for. We just hope that it’s fun!
